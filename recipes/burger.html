Chicken Burger Recipe

Description

Why wait for a weekend BBQ? Make this chicken burger for dinner tonight!

Crispy seasoned chicken breast, topped with mandatory melted cheese and piled onto soft rolls with onion, avocado, lettuce, tomato and garlic mayo. Quick to make for a midweek meal, great for gatherings!

Ingredients

  • 250 grams Ground Chicken
  • 30 grams Olives
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 leaves of lettuce
  • salt (to your liking)
  • spicy sauce (to your liking)

Steps

1. Finely chop the olives and mix in a bowl with the ground chicken and salt

2. Heat some oil in a pan and after shaping 2 chicken patties using either your hands or a burger mold place them in the hot oil. A handy tip is to wet your hands or the mold to prevent sticking. Ground chicken is generally a lot stickier than beef or pork.

3. Fry the patty for 3-4 min of each side on a medium heat till cooked through. Then place the patties between two sliecs of bread, lettuce and sauce. Then enjoy!
